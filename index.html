Research

My research covers a broad spectrum of civil engineering and machine learning, where I have dedicated my career to advancing the intersection of these fields. Through innovative projects and collaborations, I have contributed significantly to the development of cutting-edge solutions for the civil engineering industry.

One of my notable achievements in the realm of research is securing research funding as a Principal Investigator (PI) and co-PI, with a total grant amount exceeding $406,000 and more than 5 million RMB. These funds have been instrumental in supporting my research endeavors and have allowed me to explore novel approaches to solving complex problems in civil engineering and machine learning.

In addition to my research contributions, I authored a highly regarded textbook titled "Principles of Soil Dynamics, 3rd Edition," co-authored with Professor Braja Das, and published by Cengage Learning in 2017. This comprehensive textbook has been a valuable resource for students and professionals in the field, offering insights into soil dynamics and its principles.

My research impact extends to more than 100 referred journal papers and conference papers, which have garnered thousands of citations from peers and scholars in the field. For more details on my research publications and citations, you can explore my Google Scholar profile.
